Parmesan Crusted Chicken

This crispy baked Parmesan crusted chicken is juicy on the inside with a crunchy coating of butter, panko breadcrumbs, parmesan and parsley. Serve them with some veggies, a salad, or over a bed of pasta.

Baked parmesan crusted chicken breast is one of the easiest, crowd-pleasiest preparations out there, as long as you prep it properly. It's not hard at all and it guarantees perfectly, evenly cooked (i.e. moist on the inside, and crunchy, but never burned, on the outside) parmesan chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of brocoli florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pound of chicken breast
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
